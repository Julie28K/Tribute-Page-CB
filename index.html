Chadwick Aaron Boseman
Tribute Page

Chadwick Boseman, known for his roles in Jackie Robinson, Marshall, Black Panther and amongst others.

Interesting Facts about Chadwick's Life & Career

  • Chadwick Boseman was born on November 29th, 1976 in Anderson, South Carolina.

  • At a young age, Chadwick wanted to become an architect. His mom, Carolyn was a nurse and his father Leroy Boseman worked at a textile factory.

  • Chadwick was fond of martial arts and continued his trained into Adulthood

  • He graduated from T.L. Hanna High School in 1995.

  • He wrote his first play, Crossroads and staged it at the school. He also competed in speech and debate.

  • Instead of joining the college basketball team, Boseman chose the Arts.

  • Chadwick attended Howard University in Washington, D.C and graduated in 2000.

  • With the help of one of his Teachers, Phylicia Rashad, Chadwick got support from a very well known Actor.

  • Being accepted and with the support he was able to attend the Oxford Summer Program of the British American Drama Academy at Balliol College, Oxford, in England.

  • He had a passion for writing and directing. He was especially fond of the playwriting of William Shakespeare

  • In 2008, Chadwick turned this passion into reality with a written screenplay, called Deep Azure

  • Boseman moved to LA in 2008 to pursue his career in Acting

  • He had several roles between 2008 and 2013 , but it was his role in the Film 42 as Jackie Robinson where his career took off. His audition made him stand out to the directors. Jackie Robinson's widow even thought he resembled her late husband.

  • In 2016 Boseman portrayed T'challa from the Marvel Comics in Captain America: Civil War. That was a 5-picture deal with Marvel.

  • Boseman would go on to perform as Black Panther in 2018. Boseman was recognized in Time 100 for this role.

  • Boseman was praiosed for his work in this Movie. Black Panther was nominated for an Academ Award for Best Picture

  • His last physical appearance as Black Panther was in Endgame, at Tony's Funeral

  • Sadly in 2016 Boseman was diagnosed with Stage III Colon Cancer. Only a handful of non-family members knew of his diagnosis.

  • By 2020 it had progressed to Stage . Even through treatment, Boseman continued to work and completed several films.

  • On August 28, 2020 Chadwick Boseman passed away from colon cancer. He was at home with his family by his side.

  • On September 4, 2020 CHadwick Boseman's memorial service was held in Anderson, South Carolina.

  • Following his death, many fellow actors and friends paid tribute to Chadwick on social media.

  • Chadwick Boseman will truly be missed. He was a great actor and will be remembered for his works and the life he led.
